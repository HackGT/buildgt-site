FAQ’s

What is a hackathon

Hackathons are events where teams of students spend a weekend utilizing resources and support that we provide to bring their ideas to life. These projects range from mobile applications to drones to embedded hardware and more!

What if I don't have a team

We will have a mixer at the start of the event to help you find a team! Additionally, we will be making a Facebook group for attendees to share their ideas and find a team before the event.

How long is the hackathon?

This year's event is from 9am to 9pm on March 10th.

About Us

HackGT is a 501(c)(4) nonprofit and student organization consisting of Georgia Tech undergraduate students focused on being the drivers of computer science outreach in the Georgia Tech, state, and national communities. Through throwing large-scale collegiate hackathons of upwards of 1000 people to small-scale hackathons and workshops, our organization aims to spread the spirit of application, innovation, and creativity in computer science education.
